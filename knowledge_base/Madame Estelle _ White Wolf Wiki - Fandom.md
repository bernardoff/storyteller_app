First appearing in Marseille in

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/1655\_(CofD)

with a hobgoblin and changeling entourage, Estelle fled to France in the wake of the

Thirty Years’ War

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thirty\_Years%E2%80%99\_War

. From there, she traveled across the country with her crew, refusing to tie herself to any particular

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Freehold\_(CTL)

. Estelle built up an impressive reputation for helping the changeling community, garnering a network of allies, which she would eventually leverage into an agreement known as the

Accord of the Spheres

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Accord\_of\_the\_Spheres

, which established the

Court of the North Star

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Court\_of\_the\_North\_Star

Madame Estelle would continue to exercise her power as Empress of the North Star from her

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Hollow\_(CTL)

, Brocéliande, where she held court among the freeholds’ ambassadors.
