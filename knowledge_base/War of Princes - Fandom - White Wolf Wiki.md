War of Princes

is the period of conflict in

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Cainite

history that began shortly after the fall of

Constantinople

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Constantinople\_(WOD)

Fourth Crusade

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Bitter\_Crusade\_(event)

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/1204\_(WOD)

. The term refers to the conflicts over territory that the various

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Prince\_(VTM)

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Europe\_(WOD)

fought after the end of the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Long\_Night\_(VTDA)

The War of Princes began shortly before the

Inquisition

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Inquisition\_(WOD)

was formed. Although the Inquisitors were initially used as tools by the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Elders

, they soon became a greater threat to the Cainites than the Cainites were to one another. Although it is not clear exactly when the War of Princes ended, it would have likely been forced to a conclusion with the advent of the

Anarch Revolt

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Anarch\_Revolt

, if not sooner.
