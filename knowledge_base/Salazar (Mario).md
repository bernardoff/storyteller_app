Merits & Flaws

#### Flesh Eater (2-pt Flaw, Physical Infernal):

You cling to the dark requirement that you must consume the blood-rich flesh of your victim (like the heart or liver). If you drink blood without eating the flesh, you must pass a Willpower roll (difficulty 8) or vomit it up.

Storyteller note: A brilliant reason for why your ship has an "organ pit" in the cargo hold!

#### Hunted (4-pt Flaw, Social/Mental):

A fanatic witch hunter (perhaps from the mortal Inquisition or a Camarilla Archon) has targeted you. They are prepared to eliminate you and constantly search for your ship.

#### Vulgar (1-pt Flaw Social):

You are a pirate, not a prince. You have spent your unlife among the cutthroats of the Mediterranean, and you possess absolutely zero courtly grace. You curse, you spit, and you solve problems with a cutlass rather than a compromise.

#### The Mechanics:

You are famously uncouth and abrasive. The difficulty of all rolls involving social grace, manners, or delicate societal proceedings increases by +1.
