Appearance \[

Doc San was described as a five-and-a-half-feet-tall black man, dressed in formal wear, who looks all the more horrific because he is a decaying corpse. His eyes run down his face, smell like an open mass grave, and his exposed muscles are all gray and ratty and hanging off him in shreds.

Doc San is aware of the patron of Voodoo of

the same name

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baron\_Samedi

and deliberately styles himself after him.
