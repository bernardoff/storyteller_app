Relationship with the Inner Circle \[

The first Justicars were the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Founders\_(VTM)

of the Camarilla, who went on to form the Inner Circle in

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/1504\_(WOD)

when they held the first meeting to appoint their successor Justicars. The Justicars are the voice of the Inner Circle and their actions can only be challenged by another Justicar, granting them nearly unlimited power to do as they see fit. A Justicar can seize whatever resources are required, rearrange offices with impunity, and even depose of princes if they so desire. And though most Justicars are prudent enough to consider their actions when dealing with other highly influential

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Kindred\_(VTM)

, such abuses of power earn them a great deal of resentment from

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire\_(WOD)

of all ages. More commonly they spend their time calling

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Conclave\_(VTM)

to judge disputes and crimes, or simply to encourage social gatherings within the Camarilla, and oversee the war against the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Sabbat

Justicars are chosen by the Inner Circle every 13 years during the Camarilla

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Conclave\_(VTM)

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Venice\_(WOD)

. Appointments are an intense, drawn out process as each clan attempts to elevate its most talented members and likewise limit the power of the other clans. This politicking often results in compromise candidates and new rivalries, but in the end the Inner Circle decides on its own, and over the centuries several unexpected choices have been made. Justicars hold their position for 13 years, although a Justicar may be chosen for consecutive terms.
