Biography \[

Few things are actually known about Claudius, only that he grew up in

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Venice\_(WOD)

and was introduced into his father's plans, showing a great deal of trust between the two. Claudius's task was to slay

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Japheth

and usurp his power through

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Diablerie\_(VTM)

. To this end, he formed a conspiracy with twelve other

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Kindred\_(VTM)

, one of each

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Clan\_(VTM)

, that he named the "

Conspiracy of Isaac

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Conspiracy\_of\_Isaac

After the Conspiracy (mostly) succeeded and the activities of Claudius were exposed to the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Founders\_(VTM)

, Claudius was saved from their rage by his family members. Some claim he managed to diablerize Japheth, becoming a full-fledged

Fourth Generation

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Fourth\_Generation

vampire; however, that may not be true. Despite this, he continued to act as right-hand man for his father, even deputizing for his father during the

Promise of 1528

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Promise\_of\_1528

, and developed a strong rivalry with

Ambrogino Giovanni

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Ambrogino\_Giovanni

17th century

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/1600c\_(WOD)

, Claudius Giovanni discovered

Khazar's Diary

, a tome that described a way to tear down the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Shroud\_(WOD)

and unite the worlds of the living and the dead. On behest of his father, Claudius and a team of excavators traveled to

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Erciyes

, and plundered every tome and every valuable relic, torching the rest.

When he returned to Venice to present his trophies to Augustus, he found his sire in the company of the mysterious

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Capuchin

, who presented Augustus with

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Japheth

's personal notes that were bound within a

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith

. Through this wraith they discovered that Japheth had hidden the

True Vessel

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/True\_Vessel

(which would make Augustus's ascension to the

Third Generation

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Third\_Generation

complete), within Erciyes and that in all likelihood it had been destroyed during the burning of the temple. That information resulted in a terrible

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Frenzy\_(VTM)

from Augustus that claimed his son's unlife.

The Capuchin took Claudius's ashes with him as a payment for his services.

Appearance \[

Claudius was a short, stout man with elegantly tailored clothing. He usually would sport a Skull pendant.
