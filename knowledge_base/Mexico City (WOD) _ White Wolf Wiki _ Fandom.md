Mexico City is home to several rather unpleasant supernatural factions, who hide their hideous rituals in the sheer mass of natives. Most supernaturals, however, recognize Mexico City as the headquarters of the vampiric

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Sabbat

, and give the city a wide berth.

In truth, the city itself is simply too large to be controlled by one major faction and so, supernaturals from all different kind are able to carve their niche into the city - unpleasant conditions such as the high crime rate and the air pollution aside.

Mexico City was known in the

modern nights

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Modern\_nights

as the spiritual center of the Sabbat, but this was not always the case. In the time of the Aztecs, the deified vampire

Huitzilopochtli

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Huitzilopochtli\_(VTM)

and his clique of similar supernaturals had their resting places in Tenochtitlan, fed by the sacrifices by their personal priesthood. When the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Sabbat

arrived along the first Spaniards, they fought against Huitzilopochtli's clique and ostensibly managed to drive them away or slay them.

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Lasombra

, using their ties to the Spaniards, used to claim the city as their domain, defending the city and New Spain in general against encroachment from the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Setites

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Giovanni

, who were major players in the lucrative slave trade of the area. When the Lasombra were distracted by territorial conflicts, the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Camarilla\_(VTM)

used the opportunity to use

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul\_(VTM)

proxies to insert themselves in the city, slowly wresting control away from the Lasombra while attacking the financial backers of the Lasombra contacts within Spain itself.

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/1767\_(WOD)

, the Jesuits were expelled from the city by order of King Charles III, robbing the Lasombra of their main asset in the city, who blamed the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Tzimisce

. Arguments got out of control and resulted in the

First Sabbat Civil War

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/First\_Sabbat\_Civil\_War

, which was used by a happy Camarilla to install their first

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Prince\_(VTM)

in Mexico City. Their rule, however, was short-lived, after their main linchpin, Charles III, died and his successor declared war on

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/France\_(WOD)

, wasting resources that the Camarilla had needed for stabilizing their hold on Mexico City. In

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/1793\_(WOD)

, most of the important Camarilla Kindred had abandoned the city, leaving only a skeleton crew of witless childer behind to hold their backs.

, Regent of the Sabbat and architect of the Purchase Pact, worked to make Mexico City the sect's new headquarters, an ideal of Cainite freedom for all to see.

Organization \[

The Sabbat in Mexico City is divided into several

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Pack\_(VTM)

that claim different quarters of the city as their territory. At least 13 major packs roam the city, and many more come and go every night, merely paying a visit or destroying themselves in

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Monomacy\_(VTM)

had their prime

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Chantry\_(VTM)

University of the Third Circle of the Golden Serpent

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/University\_of\_the\_Third\_Circle\_of\_the\_Golden\_Serpent

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Goratrix

gone, the chantry is open to exploration, if only the entrances can be found. The Tzimisce have a family branch of

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Grimaldi

– the Salamanca – settled within the city to aid the Sabbat in its endeavor.

Above the packs stands the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Consistory

, a body made up of various

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Cardinal\_(VTM)

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Prisci\_(Sabbat)

from all Sabbat

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Diocese

around the world. Not every member of the Consistory, however, attends every meeting. The permanent members of the Consistory maintain offices that would not be out of place in a Camarilla city; the higher Sabbat ranks are fully aware of the threat that a major breach of the Masquerade would pose for the sect. As a result, they try to minimize the damage done to it by younger packs by influencing and lobbying the authorities, often with force. Above the Consistory stands the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Regent\_(Sabbat)

, who is protected by at least one

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Seraph\_(VTM)

who remains within Mexico City to act as her bodyguard.

Changelings \[

Mexico City is part of the

Kingdom of the Feathered Serpent

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Kingdom\_(CTD)

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Concordia\_(CTD)

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Nunnehi

Shadow Court

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Shadow\_Court

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Topaz\_(CTD)

Society of Leopold

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Society\_of\_Leopold

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/True\_Faith

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Arcanum\_(WOD)
