#### Discipline Powers Detailed:

#### Whispers to the Soul (The Path of the Twilight Garden - Level 1):

Merely hearing the whisper of the true name of the Dark Mother is enough to drive mortals mad, and fill the hearts of Cainites with terror. System: The invoker whispers a secret name of Lilith. The target must immediately make a Willpower roll (difficulty 8) or be tormented by nightmares, losing two dice from all dice pools for a number of days equal to the invoker's Manipulation score.

#### Kiss of the Dark Mother (The Path of the Twilight Garden - Level 2):

The Lilin bites her tongue and fills her mouth with a foul, acrid mix of black bile and vitae. System: Spend a blood point. The next bite attack deals double damage before soak is applied.

#### Typhonic Avatar (Serpentis - Level 4):

The Setite can become a Typhonic Beast (a tall jackal with a hard, forked, and spiked tail) or a hybrid human/serpent form with a long, prehensile tail. System: Spend a blood point. Gains two dots to Strength, Dexterity, and Stamina. The animal form moves at twice the vampire's speed and does two additional dice of bite damage. The hybrid form's tail can act as an additional attack.
