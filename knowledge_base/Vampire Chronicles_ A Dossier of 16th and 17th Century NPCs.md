# Vampire Chronicles: A Dossier of 16th and 17th Century NPCs

Based on the archives of the

Transylvania Chronicles

, and the histories of the

War of Princes

, here is a comprehensive dossier of Non-Player Characters active during the 16th (1500s) and 17th (1600s) centuries.

This era is the crucible of the Sects. The Sabbat is coalescing from the fires of the Anarch Revolt, hardening into a military machine, while the Camarilla ossifies its hierarchy to survive the Inquisition and the Turkish invasion.

The Sword of Caine (The Emerging Sabbat)

The 16th and 17th centuries are the Sabbat’s "teenage years"—violent, fanatical, and prone to civil war before the Purchase Pact of 1801.
