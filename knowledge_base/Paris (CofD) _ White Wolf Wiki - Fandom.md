Île-de-France

, which lies along the river Seine, is the capital and most populous city in  France .

One of Europe’s major centers of commerce and culture, Paris makes up around 18% of France’s population

and houses such cultural keystones as the Arc de Triomphe, the Louvre, and the Eiffel Tower.

It is divided into twenty administrative districts, known as

arrondissements municipaux

, each of which has its own mayor.
