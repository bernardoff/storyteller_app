#### Discipline Powers Detailed:

#### Dementation 1 (Incubus Humor):

The vampire can inflame the humors of a victim within line of sight, eroding self-control and amplifying temperament to a fevered pitch (e.g., fanning mild irritation into rage, or melancholia into crippling depression). Can last for days or weeks.

#### Dementation 2 (Soul Haunting):

A brief speech allows the vampire to touch the sacred or profane within the victim. Urges manifest as visions, scents, and sounds of divinity (angels/demons) dramatically recounting the victim's greatest fears and repressed secrets.

#### Auspex 2 (Read the Soul):

The true color of the soul is laid bare. The practitioner's mind translates the impressions of strong emotions, lost thoughts, or dark memories as colors, temperatures, or scents.

#### Auspex 3 (The Spirit's Touch):

Read or otherwise experience the traces attached to a given inert object when putting hands on it. Strong emotional swells leave stronger traces.
