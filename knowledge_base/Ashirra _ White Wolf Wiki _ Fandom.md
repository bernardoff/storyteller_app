The Ashirra were founded in the earliest days of Islam. In

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/622\_(WOD)

, an Arabian

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Lasombra

Suleiman ibn Abdullah

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Suleiman\_ibn\_Abdullah

converted to Islam and took up the task of defending the faith and spreading it to fellow Cainites. Within a few years several other converts spread the faith across the Arabian peninsula and beyond. By

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/632\_(WOD)

, the core tenets of the sect had taken shape and the Ashirra established themselves as a major power in the Islamic world. At their height, they stretched across the Middle East, North Africa and Central Asia, conflicting with Frankish Cainites during the Crusades and the Reconquista, and with

Mongol Cainites

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Anda

during their invasion of Persia and Central Asia.

It is never mentioned what led to the decline of the Ashirra. The sect spread into Asia with Muslim traders, establishing themselves on the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/India\_(WOD)

subcontinent and the Malay archipelago as far east as

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Philippines\_(WOD)

despite opposition from both local Hindu Cainites and the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Wan\_Kuei

. They even managed to establish Sultans in Mumbai, Delhi, and Lahore. As late as the

19th century

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/1800c\_(WOD)

, the Ashirra were still vying for influence in

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Egypt\_(WOD)

, the Holy Land, and the steadily declining Ottoman Empire. By the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Modern\_nights

, their influence is greatly reduced and the Ashirra have entered a truce with the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Camarilla\_(VTM)

to preserve what power they have left.

The sect is strongest in

Saudi Arabia

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Saudi\_Arabia\_(WOD)

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Iran\_(WOD)

Afghanistan

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Afghanistan\_(WOD)

, though it has some influence in

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Egypt\_(WOD)
