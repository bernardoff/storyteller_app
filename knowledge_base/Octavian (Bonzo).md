#### History & Awakening:

Octavian's mortal life was shattered when a massive Mongol invasion swept through central Hungary, devastating the Transylvania region. The city of Hermannstadt was completely decimated, claiming the lives of many in the Mulbach family, including his brother Edward. Miraculously, the Mongol forces bypassed his city of Mulbach, leaving only Octavian, his brother Geraldt, and two sisters as survivors. Feeling utterly impotent, he isolated himself, desperately searching for ways to protect his family. During a night of delirium, a voice called to him—taking the form of a faceless woman. Unable to resist, he followed her up the Olt River to Mount Moldoveanu. There, bathed in moonlight, he found an immense castle and the woman, who introduced herself as "Miss Fanchom". The castle was Ceoris, the legendary chantry of Clan Tremere. Octavian learned that the voice he had been hearing was his own avatar—a connection to his soul allowing the manipulation of true magicka, awakened by his profound emotional trauma.

Thaumaturgical Rituals

#### Rite of Introduction (Level 1):

The formal Tremere method of presentation for arrivals in a new city. The caster boils ground tamarisk root and galangal oil in rainwater to telepathically communicate a 30-second message to the local Regent.

#### Donning the Mask of Shadows (Level 2):

Renders the subject's form translucent and smoky while muffling their sounds.

Bind the Familiar (level 3): Bind a familiar-kind of creature into your service.

#### Scrying (level 4):

Allows the caster to use a bowl of water or blood to center on a person or location and see/hear as if they were there (requires a personal item of the target).

#### Abandon the Fetters (Level 5):

A grueling, night-long ritual that completely destroys a Blood Oath by evaporating the regnant's blood within the subject, inflicting unsoakable aggravated damage in the process.
