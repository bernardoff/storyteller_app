Merits & Flaws

#### Patron (3 pts, Social):

You are fortunate enough to have a personage of some wealth and power who is willing to sponsor your endeavors. Presumably, your Patron is fond of you and is willing to offer you material goods to further whatever agreed upon goal you may have, so long as you do not abuse their favor. The scale of this Merit pertains both to the power of your Patron and how close they are to you. A 1-point Patron could be a distant but powerful vampire... A 3-point Patron might be a mortal prince or queen, or a vampire of some standing more willing to regularly fund or champion you.

Valerian de Sforza

(Toreador Antitribu, 9ª Geração, 320 anos, Sabbat) é um Mecenas refinado e curioso (Visionary/Bon Vivant) que crê que "a verdadeira arte nasce da dor e da transformação." Patrocina escultores, alquimistas e Tzimisce que usam Vicissitude como "arte viva."

Ele vê seu Tzimisce como "artista grotesco" e potencial gênio. Oferece

dinheiro, refúgio, cobaias e proteção

em troca de assistir e usar as criações em espetáculos do Sabbat.

Ganchos: Pede criação de Szlachta, transformação de artistas ou moldar inimigos capturados.

#### Smell of the Grave (1 pt, Physical):

The cloying scent of fresh, wet earth and decay clings to you. Your skin smells of chrism (or any other oil used in a burial rite) and your breath of funerary incense. While this bothers your Cainite peers little, it unsettles mortals, thereby increasing the difficulty to Social rolls with mortals by one.

#### Monstrous (3 points):

When you were Embraced, the Beast within tore its way out, permanently manifesting itself in your physical features. You are a terrible, twisted creature, the stuff of nightmares in unliving flesh. Ordinary mortals flee in terror at your approach and even Cainites recoil in disgust. Your Appearance is permanently 0 and cannot be raised. If your character is of Clan Nosferatu, you may not take this Flaw.

#### Rivalry (2 pts, Social):

Someone hates you so much that they cannot bear to leave you alone. The scale of this Flaw is similar to that of the Enemy Flaw, but your Rival is not interested in seeing you dead. Rather, a Rival desires only to make your unlife as troublesome and hellish as possible. At lower point value, the Rival may also be a friend who arrives at precisely the worst moment to steal your thunder or make you embarrassed.

#### Touch of Frost (1 pt, Supernatural):

A nimbus of cold surrounds you. At the slightest touch, plants wither and die. If you stand near still water for too long, it begins to freeze. This makes you very easy to find, increasing the difficulty of rolls to stay hidden or concealed by one.

Szlachta (Shock Trooper)

Concept: The quintessential Tzimisce guard. Constantin takes a mortal captive, feeds them Sabbat blood, and meticulously strips away their humanity to create a living weapon devoid of fear or hesitation.

Practical Modifications: Constantin seals the victim's lips and ears with flesh to prevent them from hearing pleas for mercy or screaming. He extracts the radius and ulna bones of their forearms, snapping them through the wrists to create permanent, naturally attached bone-blades. He shifts their body fat and muscle mass to their upper body for maximum striking power.

Attributes: Strength 5, Dexterity 3, Stamina 4, Charisma 0, Manipulation 0, Appearance 0, Perception 2, Intelligence 1, Wits 2

Abilities: Athletics 2, Awareness 2, Brawl 4, Melee (Bone Blades) 4, Intimidation 4, Survival 2

Disciplines (Ghoul): Potence 1, Fortitude 1

#### Custom Vicissitude Traits:

Osseous Blades: Arms end in bone swords. Damage: Strength + 2 Lethal. They cannot be disarmed.

Deadened Nerves: Constantin has severed their pain receptors. They ignore wound penalties until they reach the Mauled (-2) health level.

Health Levels: OK, OK, -1, -1, -2, -5, Incapacitated
