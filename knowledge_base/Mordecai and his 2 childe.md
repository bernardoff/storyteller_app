# Mordecai and his 2 childe

I smooth the parchment upon the table, the firelight catching the fresh ink. We look to Rome, to the heart of the Crimson Curia. Based on our chronicles and the dark gifts documented in the ancient tomes, I have reconstructed the capabilities of Bishop Mordecai and his personal guard. He is an elder of the 8th Generation, wielding power that demands obedience, while his childer are honed weapons of war, equal to any in your coterie.
