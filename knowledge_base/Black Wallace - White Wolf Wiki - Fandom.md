Biography \[

In his mortal days, Wallace was a member of the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Britain\_(WOD)

parliament, known for both his excesses and his aristocratic demeanor. When Cromwell began his campaign against the Anglican church, Wallace joined in, leading one of Cromwell's most terrible mobs personally.

Thirty Years' War

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thirty\_Years%27\_War

, he joined in immediately, eventually negotiating substantial holdings in

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Spain\_(WOD)

Peace of the Pyrenees

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty\_of\_the\_Pyrenees

. On a visit of his new holdings, he arranged a meeting with the painter

Diego Velázquez

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diego\_Vel%C3%A1zquez

to make a portrait of him. When a servant cracked the frame, he took the boy into his estate and drove a splinter into his eye, cherishing his power and influence. It was then that a

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Lasombra\_(VTM)

who had watched him for a long time decided to

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Embrace\_(VTM)

him before a

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Ventrue\_(VTM)

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Mithras\_(VTM)

had the chance.

Despite his initially low standing in the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Clan\_(VTM)

, Black Wallace soon made a name for himself. He deduced that he could gain more prestige by revealing treachery within the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Sabbat

than fighting the

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Camarilla\_(VTM)

, succeeding when he uncovered a plot against his

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Sire

and tortured all relevant information out of the would-be-assassin.

He made it his passion to uncover treachery within the Sabbat and joined the

Sabbat Inquisition

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Sabbat\_Inquisition

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/1780\_(WOD)

, soon becoming one of the most feared hunters of infernalists and heretics in the sect. He often lingers for weeks on a punishment his colleagues have "given up on" too early.

: You are an infernalist’s worst nightmare, and often that of the “innocent” as well. Inconsistencies \[

Wallace's ascent to the Sabbat Inquisition occurred 24 years before the organization was formally founded by

Gustav Mallenhous

https://whitewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Gustav\_Mallenhous

in 1804. It's possible Black Wallace was part of a European organization that pre-dated the (largely Montréal- and Mexico-based) North American Sabbat Inquisition, or that this was a typo (supposed to be 1807 or 1870).
